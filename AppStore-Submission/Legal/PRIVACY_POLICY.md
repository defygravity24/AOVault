# Privacy Policy for AO Vault

**Last Updated: February 21, 2026**

## Introduction

AO Vault ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using AO Vault, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Fanfiction Data**: URLs, titles, authors, tags, and summaries of stories you save
- **Personal Notes**: Any custom tags or notes you add to saved stories
- **Preferences**: Reading preferences and app settings

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and app version
- **Usage Data**: Features used, frequency of use (anonymous analytics only)
- **Crash Reports**: Technical information if the app crashes (no personal data)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Save and organize your fanfiction library
- Provide app functionality (search, filtering, organization)
- Improve app performance and fix bugs
- Develop new features based on usage patterns

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- **Your library data is stored locally on your device**
- We do not upload your fanfiction collection to any server
- You control all your data

### Cloud Backup (Optional)
- If you enable iCloud backup, your data may be backed up to your personal iCloud account
- This is controlled by your iOS settings, not by AO Vault
- We do not have access to your iCloud data

### Security Measures
- All network requests use HTTPS encryption
- No passwords are stored (app doesn't require login)
- Your data never leaves your device without your explicit action

## Data Sharing

**We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.**

We may share information only in these circumstances:
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or valid legal process
- **Protection**: To protect the rights and safety of our users or others
- **Consent**: With your explicit consent

## Third-Party Services

### Archive of Our Own (AO3)
- When you save a fanfic, we retrieve public information from AO3
- We respect AO3's Terms of Service and robots.txt
- We do not store AO3 user credentials

### Analytics (Anonymous)
- We may use anonymous analytics to understand app usage
- No personally identifiable information is collected
- You can opt-out in app settings

## Children's Privacy

AO Vault is rated 12+ due to fanfiction content that may contain mature themes. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data the app has saved
- **Delete**: Remove any or all saved fanfiction
- **Export**: Export your library data
- **Opt-out**: Disable analytics collection

## Data Retention

- Your library data remains on your device until you delete it
- We do not retain any user data on our servers
- Deleting the app removes all local data

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Open Source Commitment

- You can inspect exactly how we handle your data
- Community contributions are welcome
